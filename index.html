Work

I work in software infrastructure, typically on large-scale networked systems with strict latency and performance requirements. I'm currently at Google Ads where we help enable a free and open Internet.

Before Ads, I was at Google Cloud DNS where we ran the largest public DNS resolver in the world (8.8.8.8) along with providing DNS services to all GCP customers.


Research

My research interests are, unsurprisingly, Internet performance and security. I have worked on the web TLS PKI, low latency network infrastructure, web measurement, cryptography, and online ads. I have also reviewed for:
  • ACM Internet Measurement Conference
  • Passive and Active Measurement
  • ACM Computer and Communications Security
  • USENIX Operating Systems Design and Implementation
